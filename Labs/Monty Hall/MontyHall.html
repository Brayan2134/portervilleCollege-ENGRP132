Introduction to Game

Thank you for choosing Brayan Quevedo Inc. to be your Monty Hall probability distributor. The purpose of this program (through the "Let's Make A Deal" game show) to demonstrate if the manner which you pick the game doors increases or decreases your odds of winning the big prize! Every time you play this game, data will be collected to show if the Monty Hall probability is getting closer to it's expected values.

How to Play

To play this game you shall do the following:

  • Pick a door.
  • Be shown a door with a donkey.
  • Have the opportunity to change or keep your first pick.
  • Be shown the answers!
